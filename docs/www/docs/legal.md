## Disclaimer

In the spirit of faithful and transparent stewardship, it must be stated unequivocally that all activities in financial markets carry inherent and significant risk. The Trisagion project, while designed with the utmost rigor and guided by a sacred mission, operates within this domain of uncertainty. Past performance of the Tactical Trend Trader (3T) System, whether in backtesting or live operation, is not and cannot be a guarantee of future results. Market dynamics are complex, influenced by a multitude of factors beyond the scope of any single model.

This reality does not diminish the project's purpose but rather frames it within the theological virtue of prudence. Prudence demands that we act with reason, diligence, and a clear-eyed assessment of risk. The system's architecture—which mandates a conservative approach to leverage—is a direct implementation of this virtue. The system is designed to be patient, not impulsive; to act on clarity, not on noise; and to manage risk systematically.

*Therefore, neither the one who plants nor the one who waters is anything, but only God, who causes the growth. (1 Cor 3:7)*

Ultimately, the outcomes are not fully within human control. We offer this work as an act of faith and stewardship, entrusting its fruits to Divine Providence. We commit to operating with the highest degree of technical excellence and moral integrity, recognizing that our role is to plant and water the seed; the growth comes from God.
