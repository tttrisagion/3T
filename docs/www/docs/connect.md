# Connect & Collaborate

The Trisagion project stands at the confluence of faith and technology, of prayer and purpose, of ancient tradition and modern innovation. It is an ambitious undertaking that requires a diverse coalition of talents and vocations. 

We extend an open invitation to all who are drawn to this mission—*Veni et Vide*, "Come and See."

---

## Who We Seek to Collaborate With

* **Developers and AI Researchers:** Proficient in distributed systems, Python, C++, Celery, and real-time optimization frameworks who wish to contribute to a project with a transcendent purpose.
* **Quantitative Finance Professionals:** Who see the potential for their analytical and risk management skills to serve a mission beyond pure profit.
* **Theologians and Ethicists:** Who can provide guidance and ensure the project remains firmly rooted in Catholic social, moral, and canonical teachings.
* **Supporters and Intercessors:** Who believe in the power of faithful innovation to address the material needs of the Church in the 21st century and who will include the project in their prayers.
