# Praying The Trisagion

![Praying The Trisagion](/img/masthead-prayer.png)

The Trisagion project is named for and consecrated to the profound mystery of the Holy Trinity, drawing its inspiration from one of the most ancient and venerable hymns of the Christian faith: the Trisagion (from Greek: Τρισάγιον, 'Thrice Holy'). This doxology, "Holy God, Holy Mighty, Holy Immortal, have mercy on us," serves as a foundational prayer in both Eastern and Western liturgies.

We ask you to include in your intentions the **350 million Christians who suffer persecution** around the world.

---

## THE TRISAGION
*Prayer of praise and adoration of the Most Holy Trinity.*

**Begin by making the Sign of the Cross.**

**Leader:** O Lord, open my lips.  
**All:** And my mouth will proclaim your praise.

**Leader:** O God, come to my assistance.  
**All:** O Lord, make haste to help me.

**Leader:** Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.  
**All:** As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

**Leader:** Holy God, Holy Strong One, Holy Immortal One,  
**All:** Have mercy on us. *(Repeat 3 Times)*

---

## TO THE FATHER

**Leader:** In the first part of the Angelic Trisagion, we pray and thank God the Father, who in His wisdom and goodness has created the universe, and in the mystery of His love has given us His Son and Holy Spirit. To Him, the source of love and mercy, we say: Holy God, Holy Strong One, Holy Immortal One,  
**All:** Have mercy on us.

**Leader:** Blessed be You, Father most loving, because in Your infinite wisdom and goodness You have created the universe, and with special love You lowered Yourself to man, elevating him to participation in Your very life. Thank You, good Father, for giving us Jesus, Your Son, our Savior, friend, brother, and redeemer, and for the gift of Your Consoling Spirit. Grant us Your presence and Your mercy, so that our whole life may be for You, the Father of life, the beginning without end, the highest good and eternal light, that we might offer to You a hymn of glory, praise, love, and thanksgiving.

**All:** OUR FATHER...

**Leader:** TO YOU BE PRAISE, GLORY, AND THANKSGIVING FOREVER, BLESSED TRINITY.  
**All:** HOLY, HOLY, HOLY LORD, GOD OF POWER AND MIGHT, HEAVEN AND EARTH ARE FULL OF YOUR GLORY. *(Repeat 9 times)*

**Leader:** Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.  
**All:** As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

---

## TO THE SON

**Leader:** In the second part of our prayer, we direct ourselves to the Son, who to fulfill the will of the Father and redeem the world, was made our brother, and in the supreme gift of the Eucharist remains always with us. To Him, the source of new life and peace, with a heart full of hope we say: Holy God, Holy Strong One, Holy Immortal One,  
**All:** Have mercy on us.

**Leader:** Lord Jesus, Eternal Word of the Father, give us a clean heart to contemplate the mystery of Your Incarnation and Your gift of love in the Eucharist. Grant that, faithful to our Baptism, we live our faith with persevering consistency, enkindle in us the love which makes us one with You and our brothers; fill us with the light of Your Graces, give us the fullness of Your life offered up for us. To You, our Redeemer, to the Father, rich in goodness and mercy, to the Holy Spirit, gift of infinite love; praise, honor, and glory for eternal ages.

**All:** OUR FATHER...

**Leader:** TO YOU BE PRAISE, GLORY, AND THANKSGIVING FOREVER, BLESSED TRINITY.  
**All:** HOLY, HOLY, HOLY LORD, GOD OF POWER AND MIGHT, HEAVEN AND EARTH ARE FULL OF YOUR GLORY. *(Repeat 9 times)*

**Leader:** Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.  
**All:** As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

---

## TO THE HOLY SPIRIT

**Leader:** In the third part of the Trisagion, we give ourselves up to the Holy Spirit, the divine breath which enlivens and renews, the inexhaustible spring of communion and of peace which fills the Church and lives in every heart. To Him, the seal of infinite love, we say: Holy God, Holy Strong One, Holy Immortal One,  
**All:** Have mercy on us.

**Leader:** Spirit of Love, Gift of the Father and of the Son, come to us and renew our life. Make us obedient to Your divine breath, ready to follow Your suggestions in the way of the Gospel and of love. Sweetest guest of our hearts, clothe us with the splendor of Your light, instill in us confidence and hope, transform us into Jesus, so that, living with Him and in Him, we can always and everywhere be fervent witnesses of the Holy Trinity.

**All:** OUR FATHER...

**Leader:** TO YOU BE PRAISE, GLORY, AND THANKSGIVING FOREVER, BLESSED TRINITY.  
**All:** HOLY, HOLY, HOLY LORD, GOD OF POWER AND MIGHT, HEAVEN AND EARTH ARE FULL OF YOUR GLORY. *(Repeat 9 times)*

**Leader:** Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.  
**All:** As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

---

## CONCLUDING PRAYERS

**Antiphon**

**All:** Blessed be the Holy Trinity, who created and governs the universe, blessed now and always.  
**Leader:** Glory to You, Holy Trinity.  
**All:** You give us mercy and redemption.  
**Leader:** Let us pray.

**All:** Father, You sent Your Word to bring us truth, and Your Spirit to make us Holy. Through them we come to know the mystery of Your life. Help us to worship You, one God in three Persons, by proclaiming and living our faith in You. Grant this through Christ our Lord. AMEN!

*I BELIEVE IN YOU, I HOPE IN YOU, I LOVE YOU, I ADORE YOU, O BLESSED TRINITY!*

**Leader:** You are our hope, our glory, and our salvation, O Holy Trinity. AMEN!
